Substitute

A Photomicroscopic Opera

Stevia is common form of sugar substitute harvested from the leaves of the stevia rebaudiana herb, a relative of the sunflower that is native to Brazil and Peru.

Under the microscope, the sweet steviol crystals reveal a realm of dramatic beauty and sadness. Substitute is a series of eight NFTs that tell an epic story of haunting landscapes and ecstatic bursts of color.

Artists Nicholas and Raphaël Demers captured these unique images with an optical microscope prepared with polarizing light filters. Substitute was created in Montréal, Canada.
