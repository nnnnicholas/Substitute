A Photomicroscopic Opera

Stevia is synthesized naturally in the leaves of the Stevia rebaudiana herb, a member of the Asteraceae family that is native to Brazil and Paraguay. Under the microscope, the sweet steviol crystals reveal a realm of dramatic beauty and sadness. Synthetic Sugar is a series of five images captured through an optical microscope with light filters.
